What is Lean Management?

  • Lean is a philosophy focused on maximizing customer value while minimizing waste.
  • Originated from the Toyota Production System.
  • Delivers more value with fewer resources.
  • Improves quality, speed, and operational efficiency.
  • Builds a culture of continuous improvement.

5 Core Principles

  • Define Value – Understand what the customer values.
  • Map Value Stream – Remove non-value-added activities.
  • Create Flow – Ensure smooth workflow.
  • Establish Pull – Produce only when needed.
  • Pursue Perfection – Continuous improvement.

8 Wastes (DOWNTIME)

  • Defects
  • Overproduction
  • Waiting
  • Non-utilized Talent
  • Transportation
  • Inventory
  • Motion
  • Extra Processing
